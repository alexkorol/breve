## Description (4000 chars max)

Jimothy is a spaced-repetition trainer for technical interviews: coding patterns, Big-O, Python, machine learning, and AI engineering.

Ten-card sessions keep practice fast. Cards you miss come back minutes later, then on a widening schedule tuned so you review just before you forget. A daily review queue collects everything due today, split into quick-fire questions and longform explanations you practice out loud.

WHAT'S INSIDE

- Curated decks: coding patterns (arrays, strings, hashing, trees, graphs, dynamic programming), Big-O, Python, SQL, ML fundamentals, LLM inference, RAG, and more
- Five card formats: flashcards, multiple choice, fill-in-the-code, true/false, and ordering
- Study mode to flip through a deck casually, session mode when you want to be graded
- A streak flame that burns hotter the longer you keep it alive
- Stats: reviews per day, readiness by topic, weak-card surfacing

BRING YOUR OWN AI (OPTIONAL)

Add your own OpenRouter or Anthropic API key to generate new decks on any topic and get feedback on spoken answers. No key, no problem: every built-in deck works offline.

LOCAL FIRST

Everything lives on your device. No account, no ads, no tracking, no server. Export your progress as a file whenever you want.
